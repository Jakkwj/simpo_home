SimpoWater

A Scientific Computing Platform

SimpoWater is a SaaS computing platform for calculating the matrix like biochemical models (such as activated sludge models 1, 2 and 3). The aim of SimpoWater is to help people to build and calculate their own model easily.

Easy to Use

SimpoWater lets you focus on your model deducing and experimental datas, we'll do the simulation and evaluation.

  • Ability to perform simulation and evaluation without programing. After installing the calculaiton client, you can just build a calculaiton by created, cloned or imported (at most 3) xlsx files.
  • The 3 essential elements of SimpoWater are: BioModel (model), DataSet (data) and Project (solution), which makes SimpoWater is highly composable: one can arbitrary combine the modles and datas only if the model's components are the same as the data's targets.

Open and Liberal

All the models and datas are easy to share and track.

  • Modles and datas can be reused and shared to the community.
  • All the algorithms are open source, the modle's structure and the data authenticity can be tracked effectively.
